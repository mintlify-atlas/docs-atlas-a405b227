## Welcome to PixelTech Colombia

PixelTech Colombia is a comprehensive e-commerce platform built for technology retail in Colombia. The platform provides a complete solution for selling electronics, managing inventory, processing payments, and delivering exceptional customer service through WhatsApp integration.
